## Synopsis

Along the river of the filmmaker’s ancestral hometown Pingli, memory and myth flow together. Between Nüwa’s creation and the noise of modernization, a personal journey unfolds, searching for belonging, balance, and the quiet beauty of water that must never fall silent.
